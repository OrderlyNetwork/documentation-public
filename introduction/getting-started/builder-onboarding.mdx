# Builder Onboarding

Use this page when your team is planning to launch its own trading frontend on Orderly and onboard its own users. This page covers builder setup and launch readiness. Once your builder identity is in place, move to the technical docs to implement the product itself.

If you are an API trader using an existing builder, you do not need the full builder onboarding flow. Start from the [Integration FAQs](/build-on-omnichain/integration-faqs) instead.

## What this page covers

This guide helps you:

- choose the right launch path
- set up your builder profile
- understand how builder fees and tiers work
- register your admin account
- hand off cleanly into implementation and launch preparation

## Step 1: Choose your launch path

There are two primary ways to launch on Orderly. Pick the path that matches your product scope and engineering needs.

### Option A: Orderly One

Orderly One is the faster path to market if you want a lower-code route and a branded trading experience with less custom frontend work.

- **Best for:** teams that want to launch quickly with a more packaged setup
- **Graduation fee:** $100
- **Next step:** sign up on the [Orderly One portal](https://dex.orderly.network/dex) and create your DEX frontend

### Option B: Custom SDK/API integration

Custom integration is the better fit when you want full control over product design, frontend behavior, and integration logic.

- **Best for:** wallets, existing exchanges, and teams building a more customized trading journey
- **Graduation fee:** $10
- **Next step:** sign up on the [Orderly One portal](https://dex.orderly.network/dex), create your builder profile, and graduate to obtain your broker ID. You do not need to create a DEX frontend. Then continue through the builder setup steps on this page and move into the [Integration Checklist](/build-on-omnichain/integration-checklist)

## Step 2: Contact Orderly and align onboarding

Start by reaching out through an official Orderly channel so the team can align with you on onboarding, launch scope, and any commercial or operational requirements.

- [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/orderlynetwork)
- [Telegram](https://t.me/Orderly_Discussions)
- [Orderly website](https://orderly.network)

<Note>
  All builders should sign up on the Orderly One portal and complete the setup/graduation process first. Low-code builders will create a DEX frontend using the Orderly One service. If you are taking the custom SDK/API integration path, you can create your builder profile and graduate yourself to obtain the broker ID without creating a DEX frontend. Only reach out to the team for further help and assistance after you have created your builder profile.
</Note>

This step is where you confirm the launch path, builder profile setup, and the practical sequence for getting to go-live.

## Step 3: Prepare your builder profile

To initialize your setup, you should be ready to provide the core information Orderly needs to create your builder profile:

- **Builder ID**
- **Builder name**
- **Initial fee configuration**
- **Launch path** (Orderly One or custom integration)
- **Distributor referral code** (if you were referred by a distributor)

Once this information is confirmed, Orderly can provision your builder profile, assign the appropriate permissions, and help you move toward admin registration.

## Step 4: Review the fee model and builder tiering

Before launch, make sure your team understands how builder monetization works:

- Builders define the user-facing fee charged on their own frontend.
- Orderly charges a separate base fee underneath.
- The gap between those two numbers is the builder's margin.

Base fees depend on your tier in the [Builder Staking Programme](/introduction/trade-on-orderly/trading-basics/trading-fees#builder-staking-programme), so this page does not repeat the fee tables. Use the canonical [Trading fees](/introduction/trade-on-orderly/trading-basics/trading-fees) page when you need the current tiers, requirements, and fee rates.

## Step 5: Register your admin account

Once your builder profile is ready, register the admin account that will manage builder-level settings and fee configuration.

### For Orderly One builders

1. Complete the setup or graduation flow in the [Orderly One portal](https://dex.orderly.network/dex).
2. If you were referred by a distributor, input the distributor code for binding at profile creation or before/at graduation.
3. Pay the **$100 graduation fee** in USDC, USDT, or ORDER.
4. Secure any Orderly keys issued during setup.
5. Complete the wallet-linking steps required by the onboarding process.

### For custom SDK/API builders

1. Sign up on the [Orderly One portal](https://dex.orderly.network/dex) and complete the graduation flow to obtain your broker ID. You do not need to create a DEX frontend.
2. If you were referred by a distributor, input the distributor code for binding at profile creation or before/at graduation.
3. Pay the **$10 graduation fee** in USDC, USDT, or ORDER.
4. Visit the [builder registration tool](https://github-dev.orderly.network/broker-registration/).
5. Connect your wallet and complete the registration flow.
6. Secure any Orderly keys issued during setup.
7. Complete the wallet-linking steps required to finish admin setup.

After the linkage is complete, you can access the [Orderly admin portal](https://admin.orderly.network).

## Step 6: Confirm readiness and move into implementation

At this point, your team should have:

- a builder profile
- an admin account
- a clear fee and tiering model
- alignment with Orderly on launch readiness

## FAQs

### Do I need builder onboarding if I am only trading through an existing integration?

No. If you are using an existing builder rather than launching your own frontend, start from the [Integration FAQs](/build-on-omnichain/integration-faqs).

### Where do I manage my builder settings after setup?

Use the [Orderly admin portal](https://admin.orderly.network) after your admin account has been linked and activated.

### Where do I find the current builder fee tiers?

Use the [Trading fees](/introduction/trade-on-orderly/trading-basics/trading-fees) page for the current builder fee model and the [Builder Staking Programme](/introduction/trade-on-orderly/trading-basics/trading-fees#builder-staking-programme) tiers.

### How do I know when my builder profile is ready?

Orderly will notify your team as the builder profile and admin setup are completed during onboarding.

### What should I do after onboarding is complete?

Move directly into the [Integration Checklist](/build-on-omnichain/integration-checklist) so the builder setup work turns into an actual implementation plan.
