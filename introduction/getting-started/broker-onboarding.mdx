# How to Become a Builder on Orderly Network

Hi there, welcome!

If you're reading this, you’re likely interested in becoming a broker partner (Builder) with Orderly Network. Becoming a Builder on Orderly allows you to launch high-performance trading applications, leverage our omnichain liquidity, and generate revenue through customizable trading fees.

This guide walks you through the full onboarding process.

---

## 🚀 Step 1: Get in Touch with the Orderly Team

Start by reaching out to the Orderly team directly via official channels like [Discord](https://discord.com) and [Telegram](https://t.me), or through the [Orderly Network website](https://orderly.network).

Once connected, our team will guide you through the initial configuration of your Builder profile.

---

## 📝 Step 2: Provide Initial Broker Information

You will need to confirm the following details to initialize your profile:

- **Broker ID:** (e.g., `my_dex_identifier`)
- **Broker Name:** (e.g., `MyDEX`)
- **Default Taker Fee:** 0.06% (6 bps)
- **Default Maker Fee:** 0.03% (3 bps)

These rates are pre-filled with standard examples—you can adjust them now or modify them via the Admin Panel later.

### Understanding the Fee Model:

Orderly utilizes a "Base + Margin" model, providing you with full pricing autonomy:

- **Orderly Base Fee:** This is the cost Orderly retains. It starts at **3 bps (0.03%) for Taker** fees and **0 bps for Maker** fees.
- **Builder Margin:** You decide the final fee charged to your users. Any amount set above the Orderly Base Fee is **100% your profit**.
  
**Example:**  
If your current Base Taker Fee is 3 bps and you set your user-facing Taker Fee to 6 bps, you earn **3 bps** in profit on every taker trade.

📖 [Learn more about Trading Fees & Revenue Share](/introduction/trade-on-orderly/trading-basics/trading-fees)

---

### 🔥 Public Tier vs. Staking Tier

During setup, you should determine your starting tier in the **Builder Staking Programme**:
- **Public Tier:** Start with no staking or volume requirements (Base Taker Fee: 3 bps).
- **Staking/Volume Tiers:** Stake $ORDER tokens or meet volume thresholds to instantly lower your Base Fee, increasing your profit margins.

📖 [View the Builder Staking Tiers & Benefits](/introduction/trade-on-orderly/trading-basics/trading-fees#builder-staking-programme)

> **NOTE:** You can upgrade your tier at any time as your platform grows or as you stake more $ORDER.

---

## 🛠 Step 3: Profile Setup by Orderly

After receiving your details, the Orderly team will:
- Register your Builder profile internally.
- Assign your unique Broker ID.
- Configure your initial fee levels and permissions.

⏳ This process typically takes **1 to 2 business days**. You will be notified once your profile is live in the backend.

---

## 🔐 Step 4: Register Your Admin Account

Once your profile is ready, you must register your **Admin Account**. This account is linked to your Broker ID and is used to manage settings and collect your earned trading fees.

### Registration Steps:

1.  Visit the [Broker Registration Tool](https://github-dev.orderly.network).
2.  Connect your wallet and complete the registration flow.
3.  **Secure your keys:** After registration, you will receive an Orderly Public and Private Key.
    ```text
    Orderly Public Key: ed25519:xxxxxxxxxxxx
    Orderly Private Key: ed25519:xxxxxxxxxxxx
    ```
4.  **Final Linkage:** Securely share your **Wallet Public Address** (not your private key) with the Orderly team.

> **IMPORTANT:** Treat your Private Key as a secret. Never expose it to anyone, including the Orderly team. It is required for signing administrative requests.

✅ Once the final linkage is completed, you will have access to the [Orderly Admin Portal](https://admin.orderly.network).

# 💬 FAQs

### What if I want to change my user fees later?
You can modify your maker and taker fees at any time through the [Admin Portal](https://admin.orderly.network). Changes reflect in real-time.

### Can I change from the Public Tier to a Staking Tier later?
Yes. You can move up the tiers at any time by staking the required amount of $ORDER or by reaching the aggregate volume thresholds.

### Can I register multiple admin accounts?
Currently, each Broker ID is associated with a single primary admin wallet address at registration.
