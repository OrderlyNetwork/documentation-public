There are many ways to trade on Orderly. Users can trade through an API or one of the integrated front-ends built on top of Orderly.

The liquidity of Orderly is shared across all applications listed on the platform. Because liquidity is pooled in one place, no matter which application you trade on, you will be accessing the same pool and orderbook.

While there is not an official Orderly DEX, there are many teams hosting graphical user interfaces (GUIs) on the Orderly infrastructure.
