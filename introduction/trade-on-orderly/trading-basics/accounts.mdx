---
title: "Accounts"
description: "Account structure"
---
Orderly has implemented an account structure where each address can have several accounts, one account per front-end that operates on Orderly. 

The account address is derived from the user's wallet address and the builder ID they are trading from, so there is one account per integrator.

Users' trading activity, such as trades, fees paid, and volume, is compartmentalized by front-end. Each account is completely independent, so trades placed through front-end A cannot be seen or modified through front-end B, even if both accounts are owned by the same wallet. Fee tiers are set per account.
