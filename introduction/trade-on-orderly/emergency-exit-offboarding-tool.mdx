## Overview

The **Emergency Exit** tool provides a simple and quick solution for users who need to close all open positions, cancel orders, and withdraw their funds from Orderly Network DEXs.

## When to Use

You may want to use the offboarding tool if you:

- Need to exit all positions quickly due to market conditions
- Want to withdraw all funds from Orderly Network in a streamlined process
- Are discontinuing use of a particular DEX built on Orderly
- Need an emergency exit for any reason

## How It Works

The offboarding process involves two simple steps:

1. **Close positions and cancel orders** — Market close all your perpetual futures positions and cancel any pending orders
2. **Withdraw/transfer funds** — Transfer your USDC and other assets to your wallet

## Access the Tool

**🔗 [https://offboarding.orderly.network/](https://offboarding.orderly.network/)**

<Note>
  Make sure you have sufficient gas funds in your wallet to complete the transactions.
</Note>

## Important Notes

- All positions will be closed at market price
- Ensure you review the final PnL before confirming
- Withdrawals are subject to the DEX's withdrawal processing time
- Some DEXs may have minimum withdrawal amounts or fees
