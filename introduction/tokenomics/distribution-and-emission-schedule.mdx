## Community (55%)

### Epochs

Trading rewards and market making rewards unlock based on epochs. One epoch = 14 days. These rewards are intended to unlock over 200 epochs (~7 years), subject to future governance changes.

### Retroactive Airdrop -- 13.3% (133,000,000 $ORDER)

Unlocked at TGE as retroactive rewards to early supporters.

### Ecosystem Incentives -- 25% (250,000,000 $ORDER)

Distributed based on measurable contributions: trading volume, user engagement, and ecosystem growth impact.

### Builder Rewards -- 8.35% (83,500,000 $ORDER)

Grants for builders based on trading volume, daily active users, new users, and other criteria. Some grants were committed to early builders before TGE; the program remains open to new builders.

### Future Product Launches -- 8.35% (83,500,000 $ORDER)

Locked until usage is formally established and communicated.

## Strategic Investors (15% -- 150,000,000 $ORDER)

Locked for the first 6 months after TGE, followed by 3.5-year linear vesting. No cliff or sudden unlock when linear vesting starts.

## Team & Advisors (20% -- 200,000,000 $ORDER)

1-year cliff (25% unlocks), followed by 3-year linear vesting. No team tokens unlock before the 1-year cliff.

## Foundation (10% -- 100,000,000 $ORDER)

Designated for $ORDER liquidity on CEX and DEX, ecosystem grants, partnerships, and related initiatives.

- 38,500,000 $ORDER available post-TGE for liquidity.
- 12,575,000 $ORDER earmarked for pre-arranged marketing partnerships over 2 years.
- Remaining tokens held in Foundation reserve with full transparency on usage.
- The Foundation's goal is to ultimately transfer full control to the DAO.
